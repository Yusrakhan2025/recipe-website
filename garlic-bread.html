Garlic Bread

Crunchy, buttery garlic bread perfect as a side or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette or bread loaf
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Chopped parsley (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix garlic with softened butter and spread on bread slices.
  3. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden and crispy.
  4. Sprinkle parsley and serve warm.
